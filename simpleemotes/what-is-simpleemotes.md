# 😀 What is SimpleEmotes?

### Introduction

SimpleEmotes is a modification that adds custom animations and particle effects for the player to use at the push of a button. Players are able to perform animation such as dancing, waving, or even a number of animations utilizing props, using a menu or customizable keybinds. Particle effects, like smoke or fire, can also be added to these animations using the menu!

### **Why Choose SimpleEmotes? A Feature Comparison with Other Plugins:**

* Unique Favorite System: You can easily mark your favorite emotes and access them quickly within a dedicated category. 
* Custom Animations: Supports adding custom animations directly from a file, integrating seamlessly into the plugin. 
* Particle Effects Integration: Enhance animations with smoke, fire, and more.
* Advanced Search Function: Quickly search for any emote by name or keyword.
